About TMSA

Our Mission

Build a pipeline for minority students into professional sports and sports-adjacent companies.

Our Focus

Networking, guest speakers, career development, and real-world exposure to the industry.

Our Community

Students who want to learn, connect, and take action toward sports careers.

What We Do

Professional Networking

Connect with sports executives and industry leaders across leagues and organizations.

Guest Speakers & Panels

Learn directly from professionals in partnerships, marketing, operations, analytics, and more.

Career Development

Resume/LinkedIn support, internship strategies, and practical skill-building sessions.

Events

Speaker Series

Fireside chats and Q&As with professionals from pro teams, leagues, and brands.

Workshops

Career prep sessions: outreach, interviews, sponsorship basics, and portfolio building.

Networking Meetups

In-person and virtual opportunities to build relationships and learn from peers.
